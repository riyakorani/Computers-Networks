# ⚙️ 3. HOW MAC FILTERING WORKS

---

## 🔁 FLOW

1. Device tries to connect  
2. Router reads MAC address  
3. Checks against list  
4. Allows or blocks  

---

---

# 🔐 4. TYPES OF FILTERING

---

## 🌟 1. ALLOW LIST (WHITELIST) ⭐

👉 Only listed devices allowed  

✔ More secure  

---

## 🌟 2. DENY LIST (BLACKLIST)

👉 Block specific devices  

✔ Easier but less secure  

---

## 🎯 INTERVIEW TIP

👉 If both rules conflict → **DENY wins**

---

---

# 📡 5. WHERE IT IS APPLIED

---

## 🔹 Connection Level
👉 Device cannot connect  

---

## 🔹 DHCP Level
👉 Device connects but gets NO IP  

---

---

# 🌍 6. REAL-WORLD SCENARIOS

---

## 🧪 Home Wi-Fi

👉 Only family devices allowed  

---

## 🧪 Office Network

👉 Only company laptops allowed  

---

## 🧪 Parental Control

👉 Block kids’ devices at night  

---

---

# ⚡ 7. USE CASES

---

- Access control  
- IoT security  
- Guest restriction  
- BYOD control  
- Device monitoring  

---

---

# ⚠️ 8. LIMITATIONS (VERY IMPORTANT ⭐⭐)

---

## ❌ MAC SPOOFING (MOST IMPORTANT)

👉 Attacker can fake MAC address  

---

## ❌ NO ENCRYPTION

👉 Does NOT protect data  

---

## ❌ SCALABILITY ISSUE

👉 Hard to manage many devices  

---

## ❌ MAC RANDOMIZATION

👉 Devices change MAC automatically  

---

---

# 🚨 9. SECURITY RISKS

---

- MAC sniffing (attacker learns MACs)  
- Spoofing bypass  
- Weak as standalone security  

---

---

# 🛡️ 10. BEST PRACTICE

---

👉 Use MAC filtering WITH:

- WPA2 / WPA3  
- Strong passwords  
- Firewall  

---

---

# 🎯 11. INTERVIEW QUESTIONS

---

## Q1: What is MAC filtering?
👉 Device access control using MAC  

---

## Q2: Allow vs Deny list?
👉 Allow = only approved  
👉 Deny = block specific  

---

## Q3: Why not secure?
👉 MAC spoofing  

---

## Q4: What is MAC spoofing?
👉 Fake MAC address  

---

## Q5: Does MAC filtering encrypt data?
👉 No  

---

## Q6: Where is it used?
👉 Wi-Fi routers  

---

---

# 🔥 FINAL SUMMARY

👉 MAC filtering = basic access control  
👉 Works using MAC address  
👉 Easy to bypass (spoofing)  
👉 Used as additional security layer
