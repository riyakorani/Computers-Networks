# 🔐 AUTHENTICATION — FAANG LEVEL NOTES (DEEP + INTERVIEW READY)

---

# 🧠 1. WHAT IS AUTHENTICATION?

👉 Authentication is the process of verifying the identity of:
- User  
- Device  
- System  

before granting access.

---

# 🎯 CORE IDEA

👉 "Are you really who you claim to be?"

---

# 🚨 WHY IT MATTERS

- Prevent unauthorized access  
- Protect sensitive data  
- Maintain trust & security  

---

# ⚠️ AUTHENTICATION vs AUTHORIZATION (VERY IMPORTANT ⭐)

| Concept | Meaning |
|--------|--------|
| Authentication | Who are you? |
| Authorization | What can you access? |

---

# 🧠 2. AUTHENTICATION FACTORS (VERY IMPORTANT ⭐)

---

## 🔹 1. Something You KNOW

👉 Password / PIN  

---

## 🔹 2. Something You HAVE

👉 OTP / Phone / Smart card  

---

## 🔹 3. Something You ARE

👉 Biometrics (fingerprint, face)  

---

---

# 🔥 3. AUTHENTICATION METHODS

---

# 🌟 1. PASSWORD-BASED AUTH

---

## 🧠 WORKING

- User enters password  
- Compared with stored (hashed) password  

---

## ⚠️ RISKS

- Weak passwords  
- Phishing  
- Brute-force attacks  

---

---

# 🌟 2. PHYSICAL IDENTIFICATION

---

## 🧠 EXAMPLES

- Smart cards  
- ID badges  
- Tokens  

---

## ⚠️ RISK

👉 Can be stolen  

---

---

# 🌟 3. BIOMETRIC AUTH ⭐

---

## 🧠 EXAMPLES

- Fingerprint  
- Face recognition  
- Retina scan  
- Voice recognition  

---

## ⚡ BENEFITS

✔ Hard to fake  
✔ No need to remember  

---

## ❌ LIMITATION

❌ Requires hardware  

---

---

# 🔐 4. TYPES OF AUTHENTICATION

---

# 🌟 1. SFA (Single Factor Authentication)

---

## 🧠 IDEA

👉 Only one factor (usually password)

---

## ⚡ ADVANTAGES

✔ Simple  
✔ Fast  

---

## ❌ DISADVANTAGES

❌ Weak security  

---

---

# 🌟 2. 2FA (Two Factor Authentication) ⭐

---

## 🧠 IDEA

👉 Two different factors  

---

## 🧪 EXAMPLE

- Password + OTP  

---

## ⚡ BENEFITS

✔ Much more secure  

---

---

# 🌟 3. MFA (Multi-Factor Authentication) ⭐⭐

---

## 🧠 IDEA

👉 2 or more factors  

---

## 🧪 EXAMPLE

- Password + OTP + Fingerprint  

---

## ⚡ BENEFITS

✔ Highest security  

---

## ❌ COST

❌ Complex  

---

---

# 🌍 5. REAL-WORLD SCENARIOS (VERY IMPORTANT)

---

## 🧪 Login to Gmail

1. Enter password  
2. OTP sent to phone  

👉 This = 2FA  

---

## 🧪 ATM Withdrawal

- Card (have)  
- PIN (know)  

👉 Also 2FA  

---

---

# 🔐 6. COMMON ATTACKS

---

- Phishing  
- Credential stuffing  
- Keylogging  

---

## 🛡️ DEFENSE

- Use MFA  
- Strong passwords  
- Rate limiting  
- CAPTCHA
