# 📶 WI-FI STANDARDS — FAANG LEVEL NOTES (DEEP + INTERVIEW READY)

---

# 🧠 1. WHAT IS WI-FI?

👉 Wi-Fi is a wireless networking technology that allows devices to communicate without cables.

---

# 🎯 CORE IDEA

👉 Uses radio waves to transmit data  

👉 Standardized under **IEEE 802.11**

---

---

# ⚡ 2. KEY PARAMETERS (VERY IMPORTANT ⭐)

---

## 🔹 1. SPEED (DATA RATE)

👉 How fast data is transferred  

- Measured in Mbps / Gbps  

---

## 🔹 2. FREQUENCY BAND ⭐

👉 Determines:
- Speed  
- Range  
- Interference  

---

---

# 📡 3. FREQUENCY BANDS

---

## 🌟 1. 2.4 GHz

---

✔ Long range  
❌ Low speed  
❌ High interference  

---

## 🧪 USE

👉 IoT devices, basic browsing  

---

---

## 🌟 2. 5 GHz

---

✔ High speed  
✔ Less interference  
❌ Shorter range  

---

## 🧪 USE

👉 Gaming, streaming  

---

---

## 🌟 3. 6 GHz (Wi-Fi 6E+) ⭐⭐

---

✔ Very high speed  
✔ Very low latency  
✔ Minimal interference  

---

## 🧪 USE

👉 AR/VR, high-performance apps  

---

---

# 📊 4. COMPARISON TABLE

| Feature | 2.4 GHz | 5 GHz | 6 GHz |
|--------|--------|------|------|
| Speed | Low | High | Very High |
| Range | High | Medium | Low |
| Interference | High | Low | Very Low |

---

---

# 📶 5. ROUTER TYPES

---

## 🔹 Single Band
👉 Only 2.4 GHz  

---

## 🔹 Dual Band ⭐
👉 2.4 + 5 GHz  

---

## 🔹 Tri Band ⭐⭐
👉 2.4 + 5 + 6 GHz  

---

---

# 🔥 6. WI-FI STANDARDS EVOLUTION

---

## 🧠 IMPORTANT TIMELINE

---

| Standard | Year | Band | Speed |
|--------|------|------|------|
| 802.11 | 1997 | 2.4 GHz | 2 Mbps |
| 802.11a | 1999 | 5 GHz | 54 Mbps |
| 802.11b | 1999 | 2.4 GHz | 11 Mbps |
| 802.11g | 2003 | 2.4 GHz | 54 Mbps |
| 802.11n | 2009 | 2.4 + 5 GHz | 600 Mbps |
| 802.11ac | 2014 | 5 GHz | 6.9 Gbps |
| 802.11ax | 2019 | 2.4 + 5 GHz | 9.6 Gbps |
| Wi-Fi 6E | 2021 | + 6 GHz | 9.6 Gbps |
| Wi-Fi 7 | 2024 | 2.4/5/6 GHz | 46 Gbps |

---

---

# 🔄 7. NEW NAMING SYSTEM ⭐

---

| IEEE | Wi-Fi Name |
|------|----------|
| 802.11n | Wi-Fi 4 |
| 802.11ac | Wi-Fi 5 |
| 802.11ax | Wi-Fi 6 |
| 802.11ax (6 GHz) | Wi-Fi 6E |
| 802.11be | Wi-Fi 7 |

# 🌍 8. REAL-WORLD SCENARIOS

---

## 🧪 Scenario 1: Home Wi-Fi

👉 Router chooses:
- 2.4 GHz → long distance  
- 5 GHz → faster speed  

---

## 🧪 Scenario 2: Office Network

👉 Uses:
- Dual/tri-band  
- Load balancing across devices  

---

## 🧪 Scenario 3: Gaming

👉 Use 5 GHz or 6 GHz  
✔ Low latency
