# 📡 BLUETOOTH — COMPUTER NETWORKS (FAANG LEVEL NOTES)

---

# 🧠 1. WHAT IS BLUETOOTH?

👉 Bluetooth is a short-range wireless technology used to:
- Connect nearby devices  
- Transfer data without cables  

---

# 🎯 CORE IDEA

👉 "Cable replacement for short-distance communication"

---

## 📡 FREQUENCY

👉 Operates on **2.4 GHz ISM band**

---

---

# ⚡ 2. BLUETOOTH TYPES

---

# 🌟 1. BLUETOOTH CLASSIC (BR/EDR)

---

## 🧠 PURPOSE

👉 Continuous data transfer  

---

## ⚡ FEATURES

✔ Stable connection  
✔ Used for audio  

---

## 🧪 USE CASE

👉 Headphones, speakers  

---

---

# 🌟 2. BLE (Bluetooth Low Energy) ⭐⭐

---

## 🧠 PURPOSE

👉 Low power + small data  

---

## ⚡ FEATURES

✔ Energy efficient  
✔ Long battery life  

---

## 🧪 USE CASE

👉 Smartwatch, IoT devices  

---

---

# 🔗 3. BLUETOOTH NETWORK ARCHITECTURE

---

# 🌟 1. PICONET ⭐ (VERY IMPORTANT)

---

## 🧠 IDEA

👉 One master + up to 7 slaves  

---

## ⚡ FEATURES

✔ Master controls communication  
✔ Uses frequency hopping  

---

## 🎯 INTERVIEW FACT

👉 Max active devices = **8 (1 master + 7 slaves)**  

---

---

# 🌟 2. SCATTERNET ⭐

---

## 🧠 IDEA

👉 Multiple piconets connected  

---

## ⚡ FEATURES

✔ Larger network  
✔ Device can be:
- Master in one  
- Slave in another  

---

---

# 🧠 4. BLUETOOTH PROTOCOL STACK (IMPORTANT)

---

## 🔹 RF Layer
👉 Handles radio transmission  

---

## 🔹 Baseband
👉 Connection + timing  

---

## 🔹 LMP
👉 Security + link control  

---

## 🔹 L2CAP ⭐
👉 Data handling (segmentation, multiplexing)  

---

## 🔹 SDP
👉 Service discovery  

---

## 🔹 RFCOMM
👉 Serial communication  

---

## 🔹 OBEX
👉 File transfer  

---
