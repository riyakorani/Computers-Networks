# 🌐 TYPES OF COMPUTER NETWORKS — FAANG LEVEL NOTES

---

# 🧠 1. WHAT IS A COMPUTER NETWORK?

👉 A computer network is a collection of interconnected devices that communicate to:
- Share data  
- Share resources  
- Provide services  

---

# 🔥 CLASSIFICATION OF NETWORKS

Networks are classified based on:

1. Geographical Area  
2. Transmission Medium  
3. Ownership & Access Control  

---

# 🌍 2. BASED ON GEOGRAPHICAL AREA

---

# 🌟 1. PAN (Personal Area Network)

---

## 🧠 DEFINITION

👉 Smallest network around a single user

---

## 📏 RANGE

1–10 meters  

---

## ⚡ TECHNOLOGIES

- Bluetooth  
- NFC  
- Infrared  

---

## 🧪 REAL SCENARIO

- Phone connected to earbuds  
- Smartwatch syncing with mobile  

---

## 🎯 INTERVIEW POINT

👉 Short-range + personal devices only  

---

# 🌟 2. LAN (Local Area Network) ⭐

---

## 🧠 DEFINITION

👉 Network within a small area like home, office

---

## 📏 RANGE

Room / Building  

---

## ⚡ TECHNOLOGIES

- Ethernet  
- Wi-Fi  

---

## 🧪 REAL SCENARIO

- Home WiFi  
- Office network  

---

## 🎯 INTERVIEW EDGE

✔ High speed  
✔ Low latency  
✔ Secure  

---

# 🌟 3. CAN (Campus Area Network)

---

## 🧠 DEFINITION

👉 Connects multiple LANs within a campus

---

## 📏 RANGE

University / Corporate campus  

---

## ⚡ TECHNOLOGIES

- Fiber optics  
- Ethernet  

---

## 🧪 REAL SCENARIO

- College network connecting departments  

---

# 🌟 4. MAN (Metropolitan Area Network)

---

## 🧠 DEFINITION

👉 Covers a city or large region  

---

## 📏 RANGE

City  

---

## ⚡ TECHNOLOGIES

- Metro Ethernet  
- Fiber  
- Microwave  

---

## 🧪 REAL SCENARIO

- ISP network in a city  

---

# 🌟 5. WAN (Wide Area Network) ⭐⭐

---

## 🧠 DEFINITION

👉 Covers large geographical areas  

---

## 📏 RANGE

Country / Continent / Global  

---

## ⚡ TECHNOLOGIES

- Satellite  
- Leased lines  
- Internet  

---

## 🧪 REAL SCENARIO

- The Internet  

---

## 🎯 INTERVIEW EDGE

👉 WAN = network of networks  

---

# 📊 QUICK COMPARISON

| Type | Range | Example |
|------|------|--------|
| PAN | Few meters | Bluetooth |
| LAN | Building | Office WiFi |
| CAN | Campus | University |
| MAN | City | ISP network |
| WAN | Global | Internet |

---

# 📡 3. BASED ON TRANSMISSION MEDIUM

---

# 🌟 1. BROADCAST NETWORK

---

## 🧠 CONCEPT

👉 One sender → all receivers  

---

## ⚡ FEATURES

- Shared medium  
- No dedicated path  
- One-to-many communication  

---

## 🧪 EXAMPLES

- Wi-Fi  
- Old Ethernet (bus)  

---

## ⚠️ ISSUE

❌ Collisions possible  

---

# 🌟 2. POINT-TO-POINT NETWORK

---

## 🧠 CONCEPT

👉 Direct communication between two devices  

---

## ⚡ FEATURES

- Dedicated path  
- One-to-one communication  
- Uses routing  

---

## 🧪 EXAMPLES

- Internet  
- Telephone network  

---

## 🎯 INTERVIEW EDGE

👉 More reliable than broadcast  

---

# 🏢 4. BASED ON OWNERSHIP & ACCESS

---

# 🌟 1. PRIVATE NETWORK

---

## 🧠 DEFINITION

👉 Owned by organization  

---

## ⚡ FEATURES

- Restricted access  
- High security  

---

## 🧪 EXAMPLES

- Office LAN  
- Corporate intranet  

---

# 🌟 2. PUBLIC NETWORK

---

## 🧠 DEFINITION

👉 Open to general users  

---

## ⚡ FEATURES

- Wide access  
- Less secure  

---

## 🧪 EXAMPLES

- Internet  
- Public WiFi  

---

# 🌟 3. HYBRID NETWORK

---

## 🧠 DEFINITION

👉 Combination of private + public  

---

## 🧪 EXAMPLES

- Enterprise cloud systems  
- Banking systems  

---

# 🌐 5. INTERNETWORK (VERY IMPORTANT)

---

## 🧠 DEFINITION

👉 Multiple networks connected together  

---

## 🧪 EXAMPLE

- Internet  

---

## 🎯 KEY IDEA

👉 Not just computers — connects entire networks  

---

# 🏢 6. INTRANET vs EXTRANET

---

# 🌟 INTRANET

---

## 🧠 DEFINITION

👉 Private network within organization  

---

## ⚡ FEATURES

- Internal use  
- Secure  

---

## 🧪 EXAMPLES

- Company portal  
- University portal  

---

# 🌟 EXTRANET

---

## 🧠 DEFINITION

👉 Intranet extended to external users  

---

## ⚡ FEATURES

- Controlled access  
- Used for B2B  

---

## 🧪 EXAMPLES

- Vendor portals  
- Banking systems  

---

# 📊 DIFFERENCE

| Feature | Intranet | Extranet |
|--------|---------|---------|
| Access | Internal | External (limited) |
| Users | Employees | Partners/clients |
| Security | High | Controlled |

---

# 🧠 INTERVIEW QUESTIONS

---

## Q1: LAN vs WAN?
👉 LAN = small, fast  
👉 WAN = large, slower  

---

## Q2: What is CAN?
👉 Connects multiple LANs in campus  

---

## Q3: Broadcast vs Point-to-Point?
👉 Broadcast = one-to-many  
👉 P2P = one-to-one  

---

## Q4: Private vs Public network?
👉 Private = secure  
👉 Public = open  

---

## Q5: What is internetwork?
👉 Network of networks  

---

## Q6: Intranet vs Extranet?
👉 Internal vs controlled external  

---

# 🔥 FINAL SUMMARY

👉 PAN → personal  
👉 LAN → local  
👉 WAN → global  
👉 Broadcast vs P2P → communication style  
👉 Private/Public → access control
